Advanced Combat Rifle

The Advanced Combat Rifle (ACR) is a state-of-the-art firearm designed for military and law enforcement applications. It combines cutting-edge technology with rugged durability to deliver unparalleled performance in the most demanding environments.

Specifications

Caliber

5.56x45mm NATO

Weight

7.5 lbs (3.4 kg)

Length

36 inches (91 cm)

Rate of Fire

700-900 rounds/min

Effective Range

500 meters

Magazine Capacity

30 rounds

Operating System

Gas-operated, rotating bolt

Safety Features

Ambidextrous safety selector

Features

The ACR boasts a modular design, allowing for easy customization and maintenance. Its advanced gas system reduces recoil, enhancing accuracy and control. The rifle also features a free-floating barrel, adjustable stock, and integrated accessory rails for optics and other attachments.

Benefits

The ACR provides superior firepower, accuracy, and reliability, giving operators a decisive advantage in any situation. Its ergonomic design and lightweight construction minimize fatigue, while its modularity ensures adaptability to evolving mission requirements.

Technical Information

The ACR is constructed from high-strength materials, including aerospace-grade aluminum and reinforced polymers, ensuring exceptional durability and resistance to harsh conditions. Its advanced coating protects against corrosion and wear, extending the rifle's service life.
